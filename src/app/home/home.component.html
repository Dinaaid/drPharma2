Information on Medicines?!

Information about what is between the medicine Originator / Brand-name Medicine and Generic Medicine

Originator / Brand-name Medicine

It is the innovator medicine created by a drug commpany after acostly procedure of development and research and the company holds the marketing rights for a specific period (patent)

Generic Medicine

Other companies are allowed to produce the medicine under different trade names, after the legal protection period (17 - 20 years). Usually have lower prices because the manufacturing company did not incur any research costs.
